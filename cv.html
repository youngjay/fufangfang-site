    • Adviser: Han Luo, Professor

  • Building time series model of Chinese GDP and forecasting, Jul. 2007-Aug. 2007

  • Contemporary Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling (CUMCM), Sep. 2004
    Problem: Planning Temporary Mini Supermarkets for the Beijing 2008 Olympics
    • Adviser: Han Luo, Professor
    • Collaborators: Meng Han, Wenyu Yang
    • Description: Based on questionnaire data, we calculated the distribution of consumer flow in the 20 shopping centers around three stadiums and established a linear programming model, by which we presented a reasonable design scheme of differently sized mini supermarkets in each shopping center such that three essential requirements were satisfied：shopping demand, reasonable distribution and commercial profit.
